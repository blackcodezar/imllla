A Collection of Private Beach Homes
By Rene Gonzalez Architects

The ultra-luxury new residential development coming to Surfside will feature eight private beach homes designed by award-winning firm Rene Gonzalez Architects (RGA).


Residences will offer refined interiors, the utmost privacy, and unparalleled indoor-outdoor living adjacent to the beach.

“Spatially oriented towards the sky, these homes utilize light and nature to inspire, elevate and connect us with the environment. They are simultaneously uplifting and grounded with the wondrous beauty that is the Surfside Community.”

Each residence will be unique, featuring bespoke finishes and materials with precise use of skylights, layered glass, and double-height ceilings to maximize the natural light.

Residences range from three- to five-bedrooms with 3,500-5,600 SF of indoor space and more than 2,000 SF of private outdoor space. Highlights include the rooftop with a plunge pool, a wet deck, and a summer kitchen, as well as an enclosed entry garden with reflecting pool.
